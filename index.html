About Us

Lenise's Cafe is a European Style Cafe with a wide variety of fresh food, pastries, and delicious specialty drinks. We use Chocolate Fish Coffee Roasters as our local source for coffee beans used in all our coffee beverages. We also serve beer, wine, and delicious Mimosas!!
